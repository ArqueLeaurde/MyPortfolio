About Me

Hello! I'm Arthur, aka. ArqueLeaurde, passionate developer from France with a love for drawing and manga.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me immersed in the world of asian culture. This creative outlet not only fuels my imagination but also inspires my approach to problem-solving in development.

I believe that the blend of technical expertise and artistic creativity allows me to bring a unique perspective to every project I undertake. Whether it's developing a sleek website, crafting a complex application, or sketching a new character, I pour my heart and soul into everything I do.
